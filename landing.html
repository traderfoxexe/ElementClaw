05 // HOW IT WORKS — YOUR COPY TRADE IN 60 SECONDS
Step 01
We Scan
Every 30 minutes, ElementClaw checks 100+ weather markets across 6 US cities. 122 ensemble forecast models from GFS and ECMWF calculate the true probability of each temperature bracket.
Step 02
We Find Edge
When our model probability diverges from the market price by 8% or more, that’s a signal. Higher edge means higher expected value. LOCK confidence signals have the strongest conviction across all models.
Step 03
You Trade
Visit the Signals page. Pick a signal with high edge. Click “Trade” to go to the exact Polymarket market. Buy the indicated side (YES or NO) at or below the listed price. Size it at 2–5% of your bankroll.
Step 04
You Get Paid
Markets settle automatically when the actual temperature is reported — usually next day. Correct predictions pay $1 USDC per share. Losses cost your entry price. Check the Dashboard to track results.
06 // QUESTIONS
How much money can I make?
It depends on which signals you follow and how you size your trades. Backtest data shows a 77.1% win rate with an average edge of 20.7% per signal. With a $50 starting bankroll and 2–5% sizing, individual trades are $1–$3 USDC. Copy traders pick their own signals — LOCK confidence with 15%+ edge has the strongest expected value.
What’s the minimum to start?
Viewing signals is completely free. To place trades on Polymarket, you need USDC on Polygon. $50 USDC is a practical starting point. You’ll also need a small amount of MATIC for gas (a few cents per trade).
Is this actually profitable?
The signal feed is live — you can verify it in real time. The backtests use real historical market prices and real ensemble forecast data with no lookahead bias. Check the Dashboard to see current performance. Past results don’t guarantee future returns, but the edge is structural — weather models are more accurate than crowd pricing.
What if the signals start losing?
Copy traders are selective — you choose which signals to follow. Focus on LOCK confidence and 15%+ edge for the strongest signals. The bot’s automated version has a circuit breaker that halts after 3 consecutive losses. Quarter-Kelly sizing ensures no single trade risks your bankroll.
Do I need to know about weather or trading?
No. Visit the Signals page, look for LOCK confidence and high edge, click Trade, and follow the instructions on Polymarket. The bot handles all the weather analysis. You just pick a signal and place the trade.
What is Polymarket?
Polymarket is a prediction market on Polygon where you buy shares on real-world outcomes. Weather markets ask “Will NYC’s high be 72–74°F?” Shares trade 0¢–100¢. Correct predictions pay $1 USDC. Wrong ones expire at $0. ElementClaw finds markets where the crowd-set price is wrong.
What’s the difference between Copy Trade and Self-Hosted?
Copy Trade means you view our live signals and manually place trades on Polymarket. No setup required, completely free. Self-Hosted means you run the bot on your own VPS ($6/mo) — it scans, trades, and settles automatically with your own wallet. Start with Copy Trade. Upgrade to Self-Hosted when you want full automation.
