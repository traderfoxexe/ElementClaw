Cryptographic proof that predictions were made before outcomes

How this works: Every trading signal is SHA-256 hashed with its market snapshot (prices, volume, condition) at the exact moment the prediction is made. This hash is timestamped and written to an append-only log. When the market settles, the actual outcome is recorded with a back-reference to the original signal hash. Anyone can verify: (1) the signal timestamp precedes the settlement, (2) the hash matches the payload, (3) the market snapshot was real.
