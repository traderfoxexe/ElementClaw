Live Signals

Real-time trading signals from ElementClaw's scanning engine. Copy trades manually on Polymarket.

HOW TO COPY TRADE:

1. Look for signals with LOCK confidence and high edge (15%+) — these have the strongest expected value.
2. Click the "Trade" button to go directly to the Polymarket weather market for that city and date.
3. Buy the indicated side (YES or NO) at or below the listed market price.
4. Size your trade at 2-5% of your bankroll. Never bet more than you can afford to lose.
5. The market settles automatically when the actual temperature is reported (usually next day).

Signals refresh every 30 seconds. New scans run every 30 minutes. Paper mode signals show what the bot would trade — the edge is real, only the execution is simulated.
